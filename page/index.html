Jurassic friends

Welcome to jurassic friends! in this game you will have to make sure the dinosaurs stay calm and friendly. You can do this by showing the correct hand gesture for the right dinosaur, but be careful, the wrong gesture will make them angry.....

The green dinosaur just needs you to tell them they're doing a good job, give them a thumbs up. The blue dinosaur will only be calmed down by petting them, hold your hand open to the dinosaur to offer pets
